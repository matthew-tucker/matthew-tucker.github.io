Welcome!

My name is Matt Tucker, and I am an Assistant Professor of Linguistics at Oakland University in Rochester, MI. My research focuses on syntactic theory — both experimental and formal — as well as the Semitic languages (esp. Arabic and Maltese). I also blog a bit about language and fencing.

(This website makes some use of Arabic and Maltese fonts. Maltese is usually fine for most modern operating systems, but if you see boxes or gibberish, you likely need support for Arabic text).
